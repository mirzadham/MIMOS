## Overview
Empower your internal trainers with a comprehensive TTT curriculum that blends core facilitation skills with hands-on AI to become certified trainers. Designed for HRDF AI trainer claimability, this 5-day course equips your trainers to:
* Analyze learning needs.
* Design outcome-driven sessions.
* Deliver with confidence.
* Leverage AI for engagement.
* Assess and evaluate effectively.

---

## Learning Outcomes
By programme end, participants will be able to:
* Conduct a Training Needs Analysis (TNA) and write clear learning objectives.
* Develop session plans, storyboards, and learner-centric materials.
* Facilitate adult learners using proven engagement techniques.
* Integrate AI tools into training activities for teaching.
* Design assessments, collect feedback, and implement continuous improvements.
* Be certified as a trainer to conduct AI training.

---

## Who Should Attend?
Trainers, L&D specialists, HR professionals, or subject-matter experts responsible for:
* Designing and delivering corporate AI training.
* Onboarding and upskilling staff on Generative AI.
* Implementing AI-learning initiatives through practical & quick adoption.
