## Who Should Attend?
* **Attendees:** Individuals who have passed PMI's eligibility criteria & intend to write the PMP Exam in the next 8-16 weeks.
* **Project Leads/ Managers:** Professionals with at least 3 years of project management exposure and aspire this certification.
* **Technical Managers:** Handle large projects & need to optimize their management environment.
* **Working Professionals:** Project professionals who seek managerial opportunities or requiring professional project methodologies and best practices.
* **Consultants:** Professionals seeking to provide better solutions to project management challenges.
* **Entrepreneurs:** Leaders who need effective management of projects to control Cost, Schedule, Resource & Quality.

---

## Requirements
* **Bachelor's Degree:** Minimum 3 years / 36 months project management experience.
* **High School/ Associate Degree:** Minimum 5 years / 60 months project management experience.
* **Post-Graduate Degree:** Minimum 2 years / 24 months project management experience.
