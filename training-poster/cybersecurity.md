* Basic computer literacy
* Experience using email, internet & digital systems
* No prior technical or cybersecurity knowledge required *(Note: spelled "rquired" on the poster)*

## Learning Outcomes
* Identify cyber threats
* Apply CIA Triad *(Note: spelled "CIA Trad" on the poster)* in workplace scenarios

* Practice effective cyber hygiene

## Event Schedule

* **08.30am - 09.00am:** Registration & Breakfast
* **09.00am - 01.00pm:**
  * **Session 1 - Foundation of Information Security**
    * Importance of cybersecurity in organization *(Note: spelled "organisation" on the poster)*
    * Information security governance
    * Risk management lifecycle *(Note: spelled "Rlsk" on the poster)*
    * Data classification & protection principles
  * **Session 2 - Understanding Cybersecurity & IT Security Landscape**
    * Overview of cybersecurity vs IT security
    * Current cyber threat landscape
    * Roles of people, process & technology in security
    * Introduction to major security frameworks (ISO 27001, NIST, OWASP)
* **01.00pm - 02.00pm:** Lunch Break
* **02.00pm - 03.15pm:**
  * **Session 3 - Computing Systems & Security Components**
    * Hardware security fundamentals
    * Software & application security basis *(Note: spelled "baisis" on the poster)*
    * Network and infrastructure security overview
    * Common security tools & controls
* **03.15pm - 03.30pm:** Afternoon Tea
* **03.30pm - 05.00pm:**
  * **Session 4 - Threats, Vulnerabilities & Security Controls**
    * Common cyber threats (phishing *(spelled "phising" on the poster)*, malware, social engineering, insider threats)
    * Vulnerabilities & risk exposure
    * Preventive, detective & corrective controls
    * Case study discussion & mini exercise
  * **Session 5 - CIA Triad & Practical Applications**
    * Confidentiality, Integrity *(spelled "Intergrity" on the poster)* & Availability explained
    * Applying CIA triad to workplace scenarios
    * Best practices for cyber hygiene
    * Group discussion, Q&A & key takeaways
* **05.00pm:** End of Session
