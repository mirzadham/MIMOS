## Who Should Attend?
* **Software Developers:** Those wanting to integrate AI tools into their coding process to boost efficiency.
* **Tech Enthusiasts:** People curious about AI's role in software development and tools like *GitHub Copilot*.
* **Backend & Frontend Developers:** Developers working on full-stack projects using AI for design and coding.
* **Project Managers:** Managers who want to understand how AI can speed up development & improve quality.
* **Anyone Curious About AI:** Those wanting to learn how AI helps with coding, from prompt engineering to code review.
* **Students & Learners:** Tech students or anyone wanting to enhance their skills in AI coding & full-stack development.

## What You'll Learn
* **Hands-on experience** with top AI-powered coding tools like *GitHub Copilot* and *Google Antigravity*.
* **Master full-stack development** by building real-world projects with AI assistance, from architecture planning to frontend integration.
* **Learn to craft effective prompts** for AI to generate high-quality code, while improving your code quality & debugging skills.
* **Explore advanced features** for improving performance, debugging, & security in AI-assisted development.
* **Gain valuable insights** into using AI to automate repetitive tasks and optimize your coding workflow.
