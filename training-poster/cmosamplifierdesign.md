# Fundamental of CMOS Amplifier Design
*Master the Basics of Analog IC Design*

* **Duration:** 4 Days
* **HRD Corp Status:** Registered HRD Corp Training Provider (HRD Corp Claimable)

## Course Overview
This training provides a strong foundation in CMOS operational amplifier (op-amp design). Participants will learn theoretical concepts, design techniques, simulation, practical implementation, & measurement.

## Who Should Attend
* Electrical & electronics engineering students
* Practicing engineers & semiconductor professionals
* Researchers & academicians in analog IC design

## What You'll Learn

### Day 1: Design Fundamentals
* Introduction to CMOS amplifiers
* Fundamentals of operational amplifiers
* CMOS op-amps theoretical design (single-stage & multi-stage) *(Note: spelled "multi-sage" on the poster)*

### Day 2: Schematic & Simulation
* CMOS op-amp design with CADENCE *(Note: spelled "CANDENCE" in this section of the poster)*
* Schematic Design
* DC & AC simulation

### Day 3: Layout Design
* Layout design with CADENCE
* DRC & LVS Rule check *(Note: spelled "RUIe" on the poster)*
* Bond-pad placement
* Back-end metal filling

### Day 4: Post-Layout & Measurement
* Post-layout simulation
* GDS stream-out
* Practical measurement
