## What You'll Learn

### Day 1: Failure Analysis & Electrical Techniques
* Failure analysis basics & workflows
* Electrical fault detection methods
* Non-destructive testing (X-ray)
* PEM & thermal emission techniques

### Day 2: Physical & Material Characterization
* Microscopy (AFM, SEM, TEM)
* Advanced defect analysis (FIB-SEM, EBSD)
* Material identification (FTIR, Raman, EDS, WDS) 
* Failure verification techniques
