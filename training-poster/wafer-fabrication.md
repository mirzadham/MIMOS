# Semiconductor Wafer Fabrication
*Build your foundation in CMOS Manufacturing*

* **Duration:** 5 Days
* **HRD Corp Status:** HRD Corp Claimable (Registered HRD Corp Training Provider)

---

## Course Overview
Gain a structured introduction to wafer fabrication processes in CMOS manufacturing, combining fundamental theory with real industry practices. Participants will gain insights into key fabrication modules & their impact on device performance.

---

## Who Should Attend?
* Engineers & technicians (entry to intermediate level)
* Fresh Graduates in electrical, electronics, & manufacturing
* Professionals entering the semiconductor industry

---

## What You'll Learn

### Day 1: Safety & Hazard in Wafer Fabrication
* Safety regulations & compliance requirements
* Chemical safety & hazard classification
* Cleanroom safety practices
* Emergency response procedures

### Day 2: Cleanroom Technology & Protocol
* Cleanroom classification & standards
* Environmental control (temperature, humidity, particles)
* Gowning & degowning procedures
* Cleanroom protocol & contamination control

### Day 3: Semiconductor Devices & IC Design Fundamentals
* Semiconductor fundamentals (silicon, doping concept)
* PN junction & basic device operation
* CMOS devices structure (NMOS & PMOS)
* Relationship between device & fabrication process
* Introduction to IC design flow (high level)

### Day 4: Wafer Fabrication Overview & Integration
* Overview of semiconductor wafer fabrication flow
* Key fabrication processes: thin film, lithography, etching, diffusion
* Process sequence & interdependency
* Introduction to process integration concepts
* Impact of process variation on yield & defects

### Day 5: CMOS Fabrication Process Flow
* Overview of CMOS device structure (NMOS & PMOS)
* Step-by-step CMOS fabrication flow
* Well formation & isolation techniques
* Gate formation & patterning
* Source & drain formation
* Metal interconnect formation
